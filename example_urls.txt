# Example Google Maps URLs for Scraping

## Search URL Examples (scrapes all businesses from search results):

1. Restaurants in New York:
https://www.google.com/maps/search/restaurants+in+New+York

2. Coffee shops in zip code 10001:
https://www.google.com/maps/search/coffee+shops+10001

3. Plumbers in Miami:
https://www.google.com/maps/search/plumbers+in+Miami,+FL

4. Dentists in Los Angeles:
https://www.google.com/maps/search/dentists+Los+Angeles

## How to Get URLs:

### For Search URLs:
1. Go to Google Maps (maps.google.com)
2. Search for what you want (e.g., "restaurants in New York")
3. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar
4. Paste it into the "Google Maps Search URL" field

### For Business URLs:
1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search for and click on a specific business
3. Copy the URL from your browser's address bar
4. For bulk: paste multiple URLs (one per line) in the "Business URLs (Bulk)" field


## Usage in the App:

### Option 1: Single Search URL
- Switch to "URL Input" tab
- Paste a search URL in the "Google Maps Search URL" field
- Click "Start Scraping"
- Scrapes up to 60 businesses from that search

### Option 2: Multiple Business URLs
- Switch to "URL Input" tab
- Paste multiple business URLs in the "Business URLs (Bulk)" textarea
- One URL per line
- Click "Start Scraping"
- Scrapes each specific business

### Option 3: Traditional Keyword + Location
- Stay on "Keyword Search" tab
- Enter keyword (e.g., "restaurants")
- Enter location (e.g., "New York" or "10001")
- Click "Start Scraping"
